                          lookback=14*86400):

    epoch : `~gwpy.time.LIGOTimeGPS`, `float`, `str`

    lookback : `float`

        the CIT NDS2 server over those at the LIGO sites

    -------
    hro : `list` of `2-tuples <tuple>`
